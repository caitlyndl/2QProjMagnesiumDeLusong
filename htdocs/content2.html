📲 Resting in the afternoon 🛋
After a long day of doing online school I start to get tired around 4 pm, so I decided from then on not to do any requirements at 4 to 8 pm to rest. I usually sleep at 4 to 6, eat dinner, and rest more, then do reqs pretty late at night. I feel more productive doing requirements around 10 pm to 2 am and I know it's not exactly the most healthy habit, but it does work for me! If you aren't a night person I wouldn't recommend staying up late at night, but I do recommend resting after studying as a reward.
📚 Doing at least one requirement each day 📖
I finish at least one requirement or two minor tasks each day and then decide if I want to do more or not. There was one time I was feeling really tired but I convinced myself to start at least one thing since I had to get work done, even if I answered one question. The next day, I was super motivated and ended up doing three projects in a day! If you get demotivated like me, I hope this system can help you if you end up trying this out. :)
🛏 Having enough sleep 💤
Now I did mention that I usually do requirements late at night, but I also did say that I sleep after online school to recharge. Even if I make sure to sleep earlier in weekends, I still end up waking up quite late but I feel so much more refreshed with lots of sleep. If you already have a fixed sleeping schedule please stick to it! My sleeping schedule is just what works for me.
